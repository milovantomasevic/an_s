• Commercialization & Market Entry
  • Phase II (Budget: 150.000–200.000 €)

Vision

Position advanexus as a scalable B2B SaaS platform for data integration and analytics, ready for SME and enterprise clients, supported by leading cloud providers.

Phase Objective

  • Production deployment on AWS, Azure, and GCP
  • Completion of microservices architecture
  • Alpha & beta testing with 5+ real users
  • Service pricing and contract model definition
  • Preparation of educational & sales resources
  • Trade show presence and partner acquisition

Technical Scope

  • Refactoring to microservices (Auth, Visualizer, Report, AI modules)
  • CI/CD deployment pipelines and scalable infrastructure (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • AI modules: SQL suggestions, trend detection, anomaly alerts
  • Enterprise authentication: LDAP, OAuth2, SSO
  • Cloud component catalog + auto-deployment

Validation Through Testing

Test Type Description Purpose
Alpha Testing Internal QA + AWS partner support Performance, security, deployment
Beta Testing 3–5 SME/B2B pilot clients UX feedback, use-case validation

Business Model & Offering

  • Tiered packages: SME & Enterprise
  • Modular pricing (core engine, AI, support, training)
  • Contract types:
    • SaaS license agreement
    • Reseller / white-label partnerships
    • Support SLAs (Basic, Premium)
  • Rapid cloud deployment & integration

Team & Operations

  • Founder/CEO full-time commitment
  • Business Development expert (B2B sales, events, outreach)
  • Technical support specialist (deployment, client success)
  • CRM system + structured support channels

Conferences & Partnerships

  • Participation in 2–3 major events (Big Data Europe, Web Summit, Slush)
  • Live demos for clients & integrators
  • Strategic networking with cloud providers and partners
  • Request up to €50,000 in additional cloud credits from AWS, Azure, GCP
