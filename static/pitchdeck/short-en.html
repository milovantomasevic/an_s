A problem growing with AI

Data runs. Evidence is reconstructed later.

In regulated companies, data flows increasingly move through disconnected systems, teams and manual controls.

Systems Core platforms, data warehouses, cloud services, reporting, AML and internal tools.
Teams IT, data, risk, compliance, audit and business teams working across different tools.
Manual trail Spreadsheets, emails, files, ad hoc checks and local evidence.
Incident When something fails, the full operational story has to be rebuilt manually.
The main problem is not execution alone. The main problem is proof.
What happened, under which rules, who approved it, and what evidence exists?
Solution
