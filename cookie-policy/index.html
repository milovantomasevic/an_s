Cookies Policy

Last update: 16 Sept, 2022

advanexus (“us”, “we”, or “our”) uses cookies on the advanexus.com website (the “Service”). By using the Service, you consent to the use of cookies.

Our Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use cookies, how third-parties we may partner with may use cookies on the Service, your choices regarding cookies and further information about cookies.

What are cookies

Cookies are small pieces of text sent to your web browser by a website you visit. A cookie file is stored in your web browser and allows the Service or a third-party to recognize you and make your next visit easier and the Service more useful to you.

Cookies can be “persistent” or “session” cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while session cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser.

How advanexus uses cookies

When you use and access the Service, we may place a number of cookies files in your web browser.

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To enable certain functions of the Service
  • To provide analytics

We use both session and persistent cookies on the Service and we use different types of cookies to run the Service:

  • Essential cookies. We may use essential cookies to authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts.
  • Analytics cookies. We may use analytics cookies to track information how the Service is used so that we can make improvements. We may also use analytics cookies to test new advertisements, pages, features or new functionality of the Service to see how our users react to them.

Necessary cookies are essential for the proper functioning of the website. Without these cookies, the website may not be able to provide basic functionalities such as displaying pages correctly, accessing secure areas, or responding to user actions such as filling out forms. These cookies are necessary for efficient use of the website.
Cookie Settings
Analysis cookies enable tracking of how visitors use the website. These cookies collect information about visits, viewed pages, and time spent on the site. Analyzing this data helps you understand how users interact with the site, allowing you to improve navigation, identify problematic areas, and optimize the user experience. In addition to tracking user behavior, analysis cookies also facilitate the evaluation of the effectiveness of marketing actions. For example, they can help you see how many visitors came to the site through a specific marketing campaign or advertisement. This allows you to adjust marketing strategies based on real results.
Cookie Settings
Personalization cookies store information about user preferences to customize the experience during subsequent visits to the website. For example, these cookies remember the user's preferred language, selected options on web forms, or display settings. This makes the website more personalized and comfortable for users. Through personalization cookies, the website can collect feedback from users about their preferences and experiences. This data helps identify areas that require improvement and adapt content to better suit the needs of the audience. Personalization cookies also record user interests. Based on viewed pages, searches, and behavior on the website, such cookies enable the delivery of tailored content and offers that are relevant and appealing to each individual user. This creates a personalized experience and enhances user engagement.

What are your choices regarding cookies

If you’d like to delete cookies or instruct your web browser to delete or refuse cookies, please visit the help pages of your web browser.

Please note, however, that if you delete cookies or refuse to accept them, you might not be able to use all of the features we offer, you may not be able to store your preferences, and some of our pages might not display properly.

  • For the Chrome web browser, please visit this page from Google: https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/32050
  • For the Internet Explorer web browser, please visit this page from Microsoft: http://support.microsoft.com/kb/278835
  • For the Firefox web browser, please visit this page from Mozilla: https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/delete-cookies-remove-info-websites-stored
  • For the Safari web browser, please visit this page from Apple: https://support.apple.com/kb/PH21411?locale=en_US
  • For any other web browser, please visit your web browser’s official web pages.
